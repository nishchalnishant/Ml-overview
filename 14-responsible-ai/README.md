# Responsible AI

Fairness, interpretability, privacy, and security.

**What lives here:** bias measurement and mitigation, differential privacy, model interpretability, adversarial robustness, governance.

**Status — thin.** Only `01-privacy-and-fairness.md` is in place. Related material remains in `../10-llms/interview-notes/` (AI safety) and should be consolidated here.

**Increasingly its own interview round**, especially at larger companies. The trap is treating fairness as a single metric — demographic parity and equalized odds are mathematically incompatible except in degenerate cases, and knowing *that* is the answer.
