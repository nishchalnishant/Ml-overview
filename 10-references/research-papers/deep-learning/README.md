# Deep Learning Research Papers

Three files organized by subdomain. Each covers foundational and recent papers worth knowing for interviews and applied work.

---

## [computer-vision.md](computer-vision.md)

Papers on image classification, object detection, segmentation, and visual representation learning.

**Read if:** You are interviewing for vision roles, working on visual ML systems, or want to understand the progression from AlexNet through ResNet, YOLO, ViT, and beyond.

---

## [llm.md](llm.md)

Papers on large language models — architecture, training, alignment, and emergent capabilities.

**Read if:** You are working with or interviewing on LLM systems, want to understand Transformer internals, or need fluency in the research behind GPT, BERT, instruction tuning, RLHF, and scaling laws.

---

## [time-series.md](time-series.md)

Papers on sequence modeling, forecasting, and temporal pattern recognition.

**Read if:** You are working on forecasting, anomaly detection, or time-series classification — or need to compare classical sequence models with Transformer-based approaches on temporal data.
