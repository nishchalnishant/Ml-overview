# 30 Days to ML Mastery: The Interview Roadmap

**Reality check:** Thirty days is a **training block**, not a magic spell — same as prepping for a big release or a season opener. Show up daily, keep notes short, and tie each topic back to something you already ship (pipelines, rollbacks, observability).

**Pair with:** [AI_ML_REVISION_GUIDE.md](../01-foundations/AI_ML_REVISION_GUIDE.md) for the **night-before** sprint, and [mlops.md](../06-production-ml/mlops.md) when the conversation turns to **production**.

This roadmap is designed for high-intensity interview preparation. It leverages the repository's **Gold Standard Hubs** to ensure you move from foundations to production-ready system design in 30 days.

---

## 🗓️ Week 1: Foundations & Data Chemistry
*Focus: Understanding how data moves and how simple models fail.*

- **Day 1-2: ML Logic & Paradoxes**
    - Study: [Fundamentals of Machine Learning](../07-interview-prep/ml/fundamentals-of-machine-learning.md)
    - Key Concept: Bias-Variance Tradeoff, Occam's Razor, No Free Lunch.
- **Day 3-4: The Data Pipeline**
    - Study: [Data Preprocessing & Feature Engineering](../07-interview-prep/ml/data-preprocessing-and-feature-engineering.md)
    - Key Concept: Data Leakage prevention, Imputation strategies (MICE/Indicator).
- **Day 5-7: Statistical Mastery**
    - Study: [Probability & Statistics](../07-interview-prep/ml/probability-and-statistics.md)
    - Key Concept: P-values, CLT, Type I vs. Type II Errors.

---

## 🗓️ Week 2: Algorithms & Theory (Whiteboard Ready)
*Focus: Derivations and the "why" behind the algorithms.*

- **Day 8-10: Classical Supervised Learning**
    - Study: [Algorithms & Theory](../07-interview-prep/ml/algorithms.md)
    - Key Concept: SVM Hinge Loss, Decision Tree Impurity (Gini vs Entropy).
- **Day 11-12: The Math of Optimizers**
    - Study: [Math & Derivations](../07-interview-prep/ml/math-derivations.md)
    - Key Concept: Backprop Chain Rule, Adam vs. SGD.
- **Day 13-14: Model Evaluation**
    - Study: [Model Evaluation & Metrics](../07-interview-prep/ml/model-evaluation.md)
    - Key Concept: ROC vs. PRC, Calibration Curves, F1-Score logic.

---

## 🗓️ Week 3: Deep Learning & Modern NLP/CV
*Focus: Scaling architectures and understanding Transformers.*

- **Day 15-17: Deep Learning Foundations**
    - Study: [Deep Learning Essentials](../07-interview-prep/ml/deep-learning.md)
    - Key Concept: Activation Functions, Regularization (Dropout/Batch Norm).
- **Day 18-19: NLP & The Transformer Revolution**
    - Study: [NLP Hub](../07-interview-prep/ml/nlp.md)
    - Key Concept: Self-Attention mechanism, BERT vs. GPT.
- **Day 20-21: Computer Vision & Multi-Modal**
    - Study: [Computer Vision Hub](../07-interview-prep/ml/computer-vision.md)
    - Key Concept: CNN Inductive Biases vs. Vision Transformers (ViT).
